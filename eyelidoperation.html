EYELID OPERATION

Eyelid aesthetics, the first signs of aging begin around the eyes. The formation of bags under the eyes, crow's feet wrinkles around the eyes and sagging of the eyelids make the person look more tired and older than they are. With aesthetic eyelid surgery, you can get rid of these problems and get a more lively, younger appearance.

Before the eyelid aesthetic operation, the person undergoes a careful examination. It is necessary to evaluate the eyelids as a part of the face and to look at its harmony with the other structures of the face. Eyebrow lift, forehead lift, mid-face lift or other aesthetic surgery may be included in eyelid surgery while planning the intervention.

Surgery Process

Aesthetic eyelid surgeries can be performed with local anesthesia for 1 hour. The upper eyelid incision is within the natural fold of the upper eyelid. It also extends along the outer edge of the eye in squinting lines. Excess skin in this area is marked by allowing the person to open and close their eyes, and the skin and muscle tissue in this area are removed. In fact, unlike the common practice, the new concept often does not require oil removal. Instead, a better option is to strengthen the septum so that the oils prevent herniation.

If only the fat pad on the inner edge of the eye is excessive, removing a little will give a good result. But removing fat pads in other compartments should often be avoided. Because although the fats taken provide a beautiful appearance in the early period, it will be inevitable to see collapse in these areas as the aging process of the people continues. In the very late period, severe soft tissue deficiency and collapses can be seen in the eyelids, which we call 'cadaver eyes'.
