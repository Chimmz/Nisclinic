LASER HAIR REMOVAL

Laser epilation is the fastest and healthiest way to get rid of unwanted hair. The wavelength of the laser light used in laser epilation has the property of being absorbed by the melanin pigment. With laser epilation performed with the laser technique with a cooling system, the heat of the laser turns into heat that directly destroys the hair follicle without damaging the skin.

The main purpose of laser hair removal is to deliver the laser beam at the right dose to the hair root, to damage it so that it does not produce hair again, and to prevent the hair cell from producing new hair. If the correct dose of the laser beam cannot be adjusted, the hair follicles are not sufficiently destroyed and the desired result cannot be achieved. If an overdose is applied than a sufficient dose, it can cause irritation and burn spots on the skin. That's why you should entrust yourself to experts. In our center, applications are carried out by health personnel under the control of a specialist doctor.
