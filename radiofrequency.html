RADIO FREQUENCY (REGIONAL ATTENUATION)

What is Radiofrequency?

Radiofrequency is a heat-controlled attenuation method. Radiofrequency is a treatment method used against cellulite, regional lubrication and sagging problems in the face area.

How does it work?

Radio waves activate water molecules and ions (sodium-potassium-calcium-magnesium) in the body. Depending on their movement, energy is released and the area warms up.

What are the effects?

Regional treatment with radiofrequency method has 3 effects.

  1. Basic Mechanism: Radiofrequency heats all tissues containing water in a controlled manner without causing it to pass (or transmit) to very deep tissues. Because Radiofrequency activates the water molecules in the cells. With this technology, fat cells with very high water levels can be reached. In other words, it can dissolve the adipose tissue. During the treatment, the walls of the fat cells are broken down in the heated area, and the fats begin to melt. The melted fats are absorbed through the blood and lymph and are excreted from the body, depending on the increase in circulation.
  2. Aged and worn collagen tissue breaks down and dies. Instead, the body begins to produce new collagen.
  3. The tissue that provides tightness and tension in the body is called connective tissue. The cells that make up this tissue are fibroblast cells. Fibroblast activation accelerates with the heat generated.
Conclusion

It arises as a result of thinning, weakening, disappearance of cellulite, recovery, removal of sagging, disappearance of wrinkles with the melting of fats, proliferation of collagen and regeneration of connective tissue.
